HEALTHY LIFESTYLESSM PROGRAMS

Running is a great part of a healthy lifestyle. But, did you know that we offer wellness programs that can supplement and enhance your training regimen?

  • Personal Health Profile — Identify opportunities to improve their health in a fun, informative way.
  • Fitness Reimbursement — Reimbursement of up to $150 of your fitness center fees.
  • Healthy Weight, Healthy You — We’ll reimburse up to $200 per year of class fees for approved weight management programs.
  • Alternative Health — Massage therapy, acupuncture, and dietetics — access a national network of alternative health practitioners that offer discounts of up to 30%. Save up to 40% on health and wellness products.
