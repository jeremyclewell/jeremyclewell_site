Finish the ten miles down Broad Street and get ten Fitness Program gym-visit credits*

We realize that when you're training for the Blue Cross Broad Street Run, you don't always work out at a gym. That's why this year, all eligible Independence Blue Cross members who finish the race will receive credit for ten gym visits good towards the Healthy LifestylesSM Fitness Reimbursement Program.** Shortly after race day, your gym-visit credits will be logged automatically into your Fitness Program account.

Not a Fitness Program member? Be sure to enroll before the race.

Enrolling is easy. Visit ibxpress.com to enroll online.


*This one-time promotion applies only to the Blue Cross Broad Street Run. Only non-Medicare members are eligible for the ten gym credits.

** The Fitness Program reimburses eligible members up to $150 of fitness center fees for completing 120 workouts in a 365-day enrollment period. Gym credits are good for 365 days from the date of issue.
