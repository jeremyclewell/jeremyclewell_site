A Healthy Sneaker

Proper care can extend the life of your sneakers.

They are your single most important — and probably most expensive — piece of running equipment. Here are some suggestions for taking care of your running sneakers.

  • Prevent odors by letting your sneakers air out completely between runs.
  • Extend the life of your sneakers by using them for running only. Wearing them throughout the day adds unnecessary miles.
  • Hand wash with mild soap when necessary. Remove inserts and laces. Air dry.
  • Change your sneakers every 3 months or 300 miles.

Be sure to check your sneakers regularly for damage and wear. Make sure there is sufficient cushioning and a sole that is in good shape. If not, it’s time to invest in a new pair.
