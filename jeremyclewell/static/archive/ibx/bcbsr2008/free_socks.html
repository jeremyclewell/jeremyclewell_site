Free running socks!

Independence Blue Cross running socks

Specially designed to help keep your feet comfortable on long runs and wick away moisture to prevent blisters, the running socks are available to Independence Blue Cross members who complete the Blue Cross Broad Street Run.

The socks are a token of our support for both your participation and dedication to a healthy lifestyle. Look for them in your mailbox soon after the race.
