10 Gym-Visit Credits for 10 Miles

Race Your Way Closer to Your Fitness Program Reimbursement

Running 10 miles is no small feat. To recognize this accomplishment and to thank you for your committment to fitness, we'll credit you with 10 gym visits* good toward the Healthy LifestylesSM Fitness Program† just for completeing the Blue Cross Broad Street Run.

Not a Fitness Program Member?

Don't worry. Just sign up for the Fitness Program by May 2, 2010, to receive the gym credits. To learn more or enroll in our Fitness Program, log on to ibxpress.com, the Independence Blue Cross member portal.

* This one-time promotion applies only to the Blue Cross Broad Street Run. Enrollment in the Fitness Program and completion of the race is required for credit. Gym-visit credits will be applied to your current fitness enrollment period within 4 - 6 weeks of race day.
† This Fitness Program reimburses eligible members up to $150 of fitness center fees for completing 120 workouts in 365-day enrollment period. The Fitness Ptrogram is available to managed care members only. Medicare participants are not eligible for the Fitness Program.
